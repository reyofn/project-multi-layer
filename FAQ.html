How can I book a table?

To book a table, simply follow these steps:

      Visit our website and click on the "Book a Table" button.
      Select your preferred date, time, and the number of chairs.
      Fill in the required information and complete the payment process to confirm your reservation.
      Click the "Checkout" button to complete your booking.

What happens after I submit a booking request?

After submitting a reservation request, the team reviews it and confirms the table's availability.Upon confirmation,
a confirmation email is sent to the customer with reservation details

Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation.
Please refer to your confirmation email or contact our customer support team with the details of your reservation.

What are the payment options available?

Cash, credit cards, visa.
